Café

What is the Café?

This is a building where you can see your friends and you can run a little café in the game.

Friends and Guests – What's the Difference?

There are two categories of members in the game: Friends and Guests. The difference between them can be confusing at first.

You can have as many Friends as you want. Friends do not directly affect your Café or provide any special benefits. Guests, however, are Friends whom you have invited to your Café. The number of Guests you can have is limited and depends on your Café's level.

Table Page

Guests can visit and sit in your Café. For each seated Guest, you earn Coffee Points, which can be used to purchase various bonuses.

After a Guest visits a Café, they must wait 24 hours before they can visit again. Similarly, when you invite someone to become a Guest, they must wait 24 hours after accepting the invitation before their first visit.

To maximize the number of Coffee Points you earn, it is best to collect them only when your Café is full. The reward increases with the number of seated Guests. For example:

  • 1 Guest = 1 Coffee Point
  • 2 Guests = 3 Coffee Points
  • Additional Guests continue to increase the reward

You can collect Coffee Points either with or without watching an advertisement. Watching an advertisement grants additional Coffee Points, allowing you to earn rewards faster.

Friends Page

This page displays a list of your Guests. It shows when they last visited your Café and when you last visited theirs, or whether you are currently able to visit them.

To visit a friend's Café, simply click or tap the blue "Visit Coffee Shop" button next to their name. You can only visit if your friend has an available seat in their Café. If their Café is full, you will receive the message: "Your friend's coffee shop is already full!" In that case, try again later.

In the top-left corner, there is a button that switches the view to a list of players who are not currently your Guests. This list includes players who are already your Friends, as well as players who have sent you a friend request.

If a player is already your Friend, you can invite them to your Café or accept their invitation to join yours. An invitation can only be sent or accepted if both players still have free space available in their Guest lists.

Redeem Page

This page displays the bonuses that can be redeemed using Coffee Points.

Hurry Up the Trucks

For Coffee Points, you can instantly advance all of your trucks by 250 km, 450 km, or 650 km. To get the most benefit from this bonus, make sure that most of your trucks are neither parked in a city nor about to arrive at their destinations.

Energy for Drivers

This boost helps your drivers become tired more slowly, allowing them to stay active for longer periods.

Premium Packages

Premium Speed
Increases the speed of all your trucks by 10% for 24 hours.

Premium Drivers
Automatically replaces drivers whose energy reaches 0%, keeping your trucks moving without interruption for 24 hours.

Premium Table
Grants access to the Premium Table feature for 24 hours. See the section below the Dispatcher page for more information.
