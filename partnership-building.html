Partnership Building

July 18, 2026

What is the Partnership?

Every activity that we do together is located in the Partnership building. This inclides: Monopolis, Season, Members, Wildcards, Contracts, Auctions.

Who should build it?

Every player who want to play with other has to build the Partnership building to level 1. Only the Partnership keeper has to upgrade it in order to expand the Partnership and to be able to have more members.

Partnership page

In this page you can upgrade the building and join to the Monopolis event. Monopolis is the map on the bottom, about this you can read in another post.
