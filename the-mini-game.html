The mini-game

July 18, 2026


What is Transport Workers?

Transport Workers is a separate activity from the rest of the Virtual Truck Manager game, which can help you develop the main game.

How to play?

You need to use the workers from each transport category to generate as much money as possible.

Click on each worker to start working, and when the worker finishes their job, the generated money will go into the mini-game account, and then the workers can start working again.

Using a sum of money, you can hire dispatchers who will automate the activity.

Each worker can be upgraded to produce more money. Every 10 levels, the worker becomes faster.

What is the goal of the game?

The ultimate goal is to buy back planes. These planes start at $1,000,000, and the price of each subsequent plane increases tenfold.

Progress can be burned to reset the price of the planes, and the planes already purchased will remain in the account.

Purchased planes can be used to receive various benefits in the main game.

Where is the mini-game?

The game has a small icon in the top-left corner with a picture of a van on it.

Tips for playing

To increase your income, you can watch advertisements. Each ad doubles your income for 2 hours. This bonus can be stacked, so every ad adds an additional 2 hours to the duration. You can also watch an ad to receive a 1-hour bonus income boost.

Once you buy a plane for $1M, it is best to reset the game. You can find the reset option under the flame icon in the bottom-right corner in the redeem menu.

You do not need to pull too much. In my experience, it is enough to upgrade only the first truck to level 20, purchase the dispatcher, and let it generate income until you reach $1M. If you watch ads regularly, you can reach this level in about 3–4 hours.

Income and upgrade costs are randomized. Start the truck by clicking or tapping until you earn enough money for the first upgrade. If the income does not reach $18 (with the double-income bonus active), it is best to reset the game immediately and start over. If you get $18 or more, continue upgrading until you reach  $50, buy the dispatcher, and then upgrade it from time to time until it reaches level 20.
