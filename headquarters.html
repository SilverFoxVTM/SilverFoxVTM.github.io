Headquarters

July 20, 2026

What is the Headquarter?

The Headquarters building is where you can manage your drivers.

Each truck can have up to two drivers. A truck can operate with only one driver, but in that case, it will spend a significant part of the day parked while the driver rests.

With two drivers assigned, one driver can rest while the other is driving, allowing the truck to stay on the road much longer. However, resting takes longer than becoming tired, so eventually both drivers will become exhausted, and the truck will have to stop until one of them has recovered enough energy to continue driving.

Headquarters page

On this page, you can upgrade your Headquarters building. The higher the building's level, the more drivers you can employ.

This page shows the maximum number of drivers allowed at your current building level, as well as the limit for the next level. It also displays the total number of drivers you currently have, along with a breakdown of how many drivers you have at each level.

Drivers page

On this page, you can see all of your drivers. For each driver, the following information is displayed:

  • The driver's name
  • The total distance they have driven
  • Their star rating
  • The license plate of the truck they are assigned to

If you click or tap on a driver, their profile page will open. Here you can see the same information as before, along with additional details about their assigned truck, trailer, and current route.

At the bottom of the page, there are two buttons: Train and Fire.

Use the Fire button if you want to dismiss the driver. The Train button allows you to improve the driver's abilities. As they gain experience and reach certain driving distance milestones, they can be trained to earn higher star ratings or unlock special skills, allowing them to transport fragile, flammable, or oversized cargo.

Hire page

On this page, you can hire new drivers.

You can hire an unlimited number of 1-star drivers. Simply click or tap on the 1-star driver, enter the number of drivers you want to hire, and then press the Hire button.

You can also spend VTP to hire drivers of any star level without any restrictions.

Every hour, a new group of higher-star drivers becomes available for hire. These drivers are visible to all players on the server, so the first player to hire a driver gets it. You can hire a higher-star driver from these hourly offers only once every 24 hours.
