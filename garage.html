What is the Garage?

The Garage is where you can manage your fleet and buy or lease trucks and trailers.

Garage Page

On this page, you can upgrade your Garage. The higher its level, the more trucks and trailers you can own.

You can also see a breakdown of:

  • The number of trailers you own in each category.
  • The number of trucks you own in each star rating.

Trucks Page

Here you can see all of your trucks. You can open the details page for each truck to view information such as its mileage, fuel consumption, engine, fuel tank capacity, towing power, next maintenance, comfort level, and mechanical condition.

If the truck is at your base, you can assign or unassign drivers and trailers. If the truck is currently on a route, you can also view the details of that route.

From this page, you can also:

  • Sell the truck.
  • Register or deregister the truck.
  • Repaint the truck.

Trailers Page

Here you can see all of your trailers. You can view each trailer's type, size, and whether it is currently attached to a truck. If you want to sell a trailer, this is the place to do it.

Buy Page

On this page, you can buy or lease trucks and trailers.

New Trucks

New trucks are the highest-quality vehicles in the game. They are all 5-star rated, and you can choose and purchase any model at any time, provided your player level is 130 or higher.

Used Trucks

Here you can buy or lease an unlimited number of 1-star trucks.

In addition, every hour a new selection of higher-star used trucks becomes available. These trucks are visible to all players on the server, so the first player to purchase a truck gets it. You can buy one higher-star truck from these hourly offers once every 24 hours.

Trailers

On this page, you can buy or lease trailers of every available type and size.
